About Us

Industrial Gas, Delivered with Precision

Our Company

PT Bingas Manufacturing

PT Bingas Manufacturing is an industrial gas company located in Batam, Indonesia. We specialize in supplying high-purity gases for a wide range of industrial applications including welding, fabrication, laboratory use, and manufacturing processes.


Our commitment is simple: provide reliable, quality gas products that keep your operations running smoothly. Whether you are a large fabrication facility or a small workshop, we are here to support your needs.
