Supply Modes

We offer a range of cylinder sizes to meet your specific needs, as well as bulk delivery options for larger scale industrial operations.

📦
Gas Cylinders
150 Bar & 200 Bar
High-pressure single industrial cylinders available in standard 150 Bar and 200 Bar configurations. Includes mini cylinders (10 Litres and below) optimized for portable welding and field operations.
🛢️
Gas Pallets / Manifolds
16 & 6 Cylinders (150/200 Bar)
High-capacity cylinder bundles complete with gas manifold systems in protective steel racks. Available in 16-cylinder or 6-cylinder setups at 150 Bar and 200 Bar pressure for high-demand fabrication.
🚛
LGC Tanks
Liquid or Gas Output
Liquid Gas Cylinders (LGC) and cryogenic liquid solutions designed for medium to high-volume consumption. Offers flexible supply integration with options for either direct liquid or gas output.
